System Under Test

UPD: After a while we made decision to convert the series of posts into an independent project: System Under Test. For updates please subscribe for RSS or follow us on twitter: @systemundertest.

Obsession with tests always forces you to look for new ways and approaches for testing. Quite often it is very helpful to get inspiration from other projects.

Having that in mind, I decided to start a short series of posts called “System Under Test”. Each article will cover testing techniques used in particular open-source project. The goal of these articles is to answer the question “How is X tested?”.

Here is a list of projects I want to start with:

  • LLVM
  • FreeBSD
  • GNU Make
  • PostgreSQL: TBD

If you want to see a post about particular project which is not on the list: please send me an email or leave a comment here with a name of the project.

If you don’t want to miss the next article - feel free to subscribe using the form in the sidebar or follow me on Twitter.

Enjoy reading!
